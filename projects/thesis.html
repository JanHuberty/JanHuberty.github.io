Master Thesis: Nutrition in Gaming

Survey of 200+ gamers. EDA, clustering (KMeans), PCA, and dashboards summarizing nutrition behavior profiles.

Tech Stack

Python Pandas Scikit-learn Tableau

Overview

This project focused on understanding nutrition and lifestyle patterns among gamers. A survey of 200+ participants was conducted, followed by data cleaning, dimensionality reduction using PCA, and clustering with KMeans to identify behavior profiles. Results were visualized in interactive dashboards.

Results

  • Identified 4 distinct gamer nutrition profiles.
  • Built visual dashboards to communicate insights clearly.
  • Provided recommendations for targeted health interventions.
